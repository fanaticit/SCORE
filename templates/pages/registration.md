# Registration Page Blueprint

## Purpose

Collect the minimum information needed to run the event.

## Modes

- Team registration.
- Individual registration.
- Individual registration with team preference.
- Waitlist.

## Public Information

- Event name, date, venue, activities, registration deadline.
- What participants should bring.
- Who can register.

## Fields

Public-safe fields:

- Display name
- Team name
- Organization or group
- Activity choices

Admin-only fields:

- Email
- Phone
- Guardian contact
- Emergency contact
- Medical or safeguarding notes
- Internal organizer notes

## Acceptance Criteria

- The page works on mobile.
- Required fields are clear.
- Admin-only fields do not appear in public views.
- Participants receive clear next steps after registration.
- Duplicate team or participant names are handled gracefully.
