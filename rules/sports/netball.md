# Netball

Netball can allow drawn league games if the organizer wants, but finals should usually have
a winner.

Default recommendation:

- Small groups: round-robin into final.
- Larger groups or time-limited events: Swiss-style qualifying into final.

Ask:

- Whether league-stage draws are allowed.
- Final tie-break rule.
- Court count.
- Match length and changeover.
- Points and goal-difference tie-breaks.
