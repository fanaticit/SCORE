# Basketball

Basketball normally needs a winner, especially in finals.

Default recommendation:

- Small groups: round-robin into final.
- Larger groups or time-limited events: Swiss-style qualifying into final.
- Very short event: knockout.

Ask:

- Overtime or sudden-death rule.
- Court count.
- Match length and changeover.
- Tie-breaks: points, head-to-head, score difference, points scored.
