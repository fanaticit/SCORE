# Football

Football can have drawn matches in league, group, or qualifying stages.

Default recommendation:

- Group or league stages can use win/draw/loss points.
- Knockout rounds need penalties, extra time, golden goal, or another decisive tie-break.
- Short community events often work well with group or Swiss-style qualifying into a final.

Ask:

- Points for win and draw.
- Whether draws are allowed before finals.
- Penalty shootout rule for knockout or final.
- Pitch count.
- Match length and changeover.
- Tie-break order: goal difference, goals scored, head-to-head, fair play, penalties.
