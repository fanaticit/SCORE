# Tournament-Day Checklist

## Before Registration Closes

- Confirm event schedule and resources.
- Confirm activities and divisions.
- Check registration count.
- Resolve duplicate participants or teams.
- Confirm privacy-sensitive fields are admin-only.

## Before Fixtures Start

- Close or freeze registration.
- Confirm teams.
- Generate or review fixtures.
- Check match lengths and changeover times.
- Assign pitches, courts, boards, or tables.
- Test public live view on the display device.

## During The Event

- Mark fixtures ready.
- Start fixtures.
- Enter scores.
- Resolve disputes.
- Keep announcements clear.
- Watch for no-shows and resource delays.

## After The Event

- Confirm final standings.
- Export results if needed.
- Remove or protect sensitive participant data.
- Note lessons learned for future rule references.
